Google Pixel 9

Click to view a larger image.

The Google Pixel 9, is the latest iteration in Google's flagship smartphone series. Powered by Google's custom Tensor G4 chip, it emphasizes AI-driven innovations and top-tier performance. The Pixel 9 continues to focus on photography, boasting a 50 MP main camera, and a 48 MP ultra-wide lens.

Running on Android 14, the device offers a smooth, secure experience. The 6.4-inch OLED display delivers stunning visuals with a 120Hz refresh rate. The phone supports 5G, and features an improved battery life, adaptive features, Gemini, and wireless fast charging round out the Pixel 9's feature set.


  • Sensor: Google Tensor G4
  • Display: 6.3 Inches, 120Hz
  • Camera: 50MP Lens
  • Ultra-wide: 48MP Lens
  • AI: Gemini
