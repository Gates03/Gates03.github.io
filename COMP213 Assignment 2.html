Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Protected areas are fundemental for the wolrd and provide many benefits to humans. Protected areas are essential for preserving biodiversity, delivering vital ecosystem services such as protecting watersheds and soils and shielding human communities from natural disasters.

The government of Nova Scotia, list the definition of a protected area as described by the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN), as

a clearly defined geographical space, recognised, dedicated and managed, through legal or other effective means, to achieve the long term conservation of nature.
