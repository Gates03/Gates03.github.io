Ciao, and welcome to "Amore", where every meal is made with love and immense flavour.

Our restaurant is located in the heart of King City, Ontario, Canada. At Amore, we combine the heart and soul of classic Italian tradition with a modern twist, offering handcrafted dishes made from the freshest ingredients.

Our menu is inspired by the rich culinary history of Italy. with our taste of nostalgia, we are proud to offer a variety of dishes that will satisfy your cravings.

Whether you're looking for a night out with friends and family, or going out for a romantic dinner for two, Amore is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious meal.
